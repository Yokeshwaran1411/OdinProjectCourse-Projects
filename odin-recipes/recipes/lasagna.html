Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian comfort food dish layered with rich meat sauce and cheese.

It’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles and set aside.
  2. Cook beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and serve.
