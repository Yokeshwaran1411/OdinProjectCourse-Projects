Fried Rice

Description

This fried rice recipe is quick, flavorful, and perfect for using up leftover rice. It's a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite vegetables and protein.

Perfect as a side dish or a main course for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chopped onions
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Spring onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Push veggies to the side and scramble eggs in the same pan.
  5. Add rice and soy sauce, stir well to combine.
  6. Cook for another 5 minutes and garnish with spring onions.
