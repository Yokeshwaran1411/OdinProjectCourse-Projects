Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are fluffy, golden, and easy to make. A classic breakfast favorite that’s perfect with syrup, fruits, or whipped cream.

Ideal for lazy weekends and brunch with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl, wet ingredients in another.
  2. Combine both and stir until just mixed — don't overmix.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Flip when bubbles form on top and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with maple syrup, fruits, or butter.
